The AI-HealthCare project aims to revolutionize healthcare in Timor-Leste by integrating smart digital solutions powered by artificial intelligence (AI). The goal of this project is to enhance the accessibility, quality, and efficiency of healthcare services in the country, particularly in remote and underserved areas.

Our Services

AI-Powered Telemedicine Solutions

Providing remote consultations and healthcare services through AI-enabled platforms, enabling patients in remote areas to access medical professionals via smartphones or computers.

AI Diagnostic Assistance

Using AI algorithms to analyze medical data, such as medical images or patient symptoms, to help doctors diagnose diseases accurately and quickly, even in regions with limited access to specialists.

Health Data Management and Analytics

Implementing AI-based systems to efficiently organize and manage patient data, ensuring healthcare providers can easily track patient histories, improve decision-making, and optimize healthcare delivery.

Predictive Health Monitoring

Utilizing AI to predict disease outbreaks, monitor trends in public health, and offer early warnings for healthcare providers to take proactive measures, improving disease prevention and resource allocation.

Healthcare Education and Awareness Platforms
